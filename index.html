Split Logic Summary

  • Court Price is split equally among all players who participated in the session.
  • Shuttlecocks share is split equally among all players who participated in the session (Total shuttlecocks used × shuttle price ÷ number of participants).
  • Calculations are done per person: (total court + total shuttlecocks cost) ÷ (total participants).
